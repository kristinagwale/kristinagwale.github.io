About the Artist

Drake, whose real name is Aubrey Drake Graham, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor. He has had a significant impact on the music industry, particularly in the United States, since the release of his debut album "Thank Me Later" in 2010. Drake has been extremely successful commercially, with numerous chart-topping hits and best-selling albums. He has sold over 150 million records worldwide and is one of the best-selling music artists of all time. In the United States, he has had 7 number-one albums on the Billboard 200 chart and holds the record for the most top 10 hits on the Hot 100 chart, surpassing the Beatles. He has had a significant cultural impact on the United States. He has helped to popularize the "Toronto sound," a fusion of hip-hop, R&B, and dancehall music that originated in his hometown of Toronto, Canada. He has also been influential in the development of the "emo-rap" genre, which blends hip-hop with elements of emo and alternative rock music.
